2022 Highlights

In 2022, Chit Chat Chai strengthened the foundations laid in its first year, deepening its impact on learners, volunteers, and the wider community.

The project continued to offer differentiated English classes, free childcare, and a vibrant community café — all firmly established as safe, welcoming spaces for women.

Learners reported growing confidence, improved English skills, and increased wellbeing, while volunteers described strong connections, reduced isolation, and a renewed sense of purpose. Shared learning experiences brought learners and volunteers together, reinforcing mutual support and empowerment.

Community engagement broadened through cultural celebrations, creative activities, and trips beyond the classroom, helping participants feel more connected to the city and to one another. Digital confidence also improved as learners were supported with basic IT skills and online communication.

Regular evaluation throughout the year highlighted overwhelmingly positive feedback, with Chit Chat Chai consistently described as warm, inclusive, and empowering. By the end of 2022, the project was not only sustaining its core activities but actively preparing for future initiatives focused on independence, wellbeing, and community development.
