2024 Highlights

In 2024, Chit Chat Chai continued to strengthen its role as a welcoming and supportive community for women learning English.

With around 25 regular learners attending at any one time, the project provided a safe, informal environment where women from diverse backgrounds could build confidence, improve language skills, and develop a sense of belonging.

English classes remained differentiated for beginners and improvers, supported by experienced and dedicated volunteers. Learning extended beyond the classroom through themed workshops, cultural celebrations, and community visits — including trips to Manchester Central Library and continued participation in International Women’s Day events.

The volunteer team of 20 active members played a vital role in the project’s success, with learners increasingly stepping into volunteering roles themselves. Since the project began, volunteering has helped several members secure paid employment, demonstrating Chit Chat Chai’s impact beyond language learning.

Cycling programmes, supported by ongoing funding, continued to promote independence, wellbeing, and environmental awareness. New collaborations — including community gardening initiatives and developing outdoor growing spaces — further expanded opportunities for connection and shared learning.

Through its English classes, café, crèche, enrichment activities, and strong community partnerships, Chit Chat Chai remained a vibrant space where women could learn, grow, and support one another in meaningful ways.
