2021 Highlights

Chit Chat Chai began as a sanctuary for women to learn English, improve wellbeing, and build lasting connections.

The project supports around 20 regular learners from 11 different countries, with ages ranging from 29 to 70 and a wide variety of educational backgrounds. Classes are carefully differentiated by language level and enriched with real-world themes such as health, homes, and cultural celebrations.

Learning regularly extends beyond the classroom. Alongside language learning, we foster shared achievement through accredited Food Safety and First Aid training. Digital confidence is grown through basic IT skills and a dedicated community WhatsApp group.

We actively build stronger communities by linking participants to local services, including art gallery visits, walking events, and cycling initiatives. Chit Chat Chai is about more than just words; it's about building confidence, wellbeing, and belonging.
