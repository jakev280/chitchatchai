2025 Highlights

In 2025, Chit Chat Chai focused on reflection, creativity, and community-led learning — strengthening what worked, celebrating learner voices, and taking learning beyond the classroom.

Evaluation and reflection helped shape the year, giving learners and volunteers space to review progress, share feedback, and plan what the group wanted to develop next.

Creativity remained a big part of the community, with volunteers and learners producing zines that captured stories, ideas, and language practice in a fun, collaborative way.

Partnerships and place-based learning continued to grow — from clean air conversations in Whalley Range to outdoor learning sessions in Alexandra Park that supported wellbeing, confidence, and connection.
