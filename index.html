ChitChatChai

Creating connection, confidence, and community – one cup of tea at a time

What We Do

ChitChatChai is a community-led initiative supporting refugee and migrant women through informal English practice, shared tea, and confidence-building activities such as cycling and social meet‑ups.

Why It Matters

Language barriers and social isolation can make everyday life challenging. We provide a relaxed, welcoming space where women can learn, practise, and build friendships at their own pace.
