2023 Highlights

In 2023, Chit Chat Chai continued to grow in strength and impact, supporting women to improve their English, build confidence, and reduce isolation within a welcoming community environment.

Throughout the year, 44 women registered, with around 20 attending classes at any one time. Many progressed into further education or employment, marking an encouraging step forward for the project.

English classes remained differentiated by language level and were enriched through real-world themes, cultural celebrations, and workshops such as clean air discussions and International Women’s Day events. Community trips to places like Manchester Art Gallery, Alexandra Park, and Hulme Garden Centre strengthened social connection and encouraged women to explore the city beyond the classroom.

The volunteer team expanded to 25 active members, with learners increasingly stepping into volunteer roles themselves — a key milestone in fostering belonging and empowerment. Cycling programmes, supported by external funding, continued to promote independence, wellbeing, and confidence among participants.

The café, crèche, and enrichment activities remained central to the project’s success, offering a safe, supportive space for conversation, creativity, and cultural exchange. By the end of 2023, Chit Chat Chai stood not only as a language-learning initiative but as a thriving, community-led network of women supporting one another.
